Monterey County is situated on California's central coast with its northern border approximately 100 miles
south of San Francisco and its southern border about 200 miles north of Los Angeles.
California Highway 101 bisects the county providing easy access to all Salinas Valley cities
and the Monterey Peninsula, while Highway 1 offers a scenic view of the California coast.

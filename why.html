Monterey is one of the cities that you "must visit" in your lifetime.
This gorgeous city welcome "all" of you. -editor-

Wonderful Places & Attractions

_____Monterey Bay Aquarium, Pebble Beach, 17-Mile Drive,
Spcial events(Cali Roots, Jazz Festival, etc.), Vineyards, national parks, Big Sur, etc. There are so many cool things to do in Monterey!

Welcoming Community

_____In Monterey, there's NPS(Naval Postgraduate School),
MIIS(Middlebury Institute of International Studies at Monterey)
and DLI(The Defense Language Institute).
These international studies(language) institutes have been built a diverse community.
People are very welcoming and have warm big hearts.

Relaxing

_____The weather is like a Fall for whole year-around.
It is dry but not too dry nor too humid.
The city has slow and chill vibes.
There are so many gorgeous beaches, national parks, and nature-friendly places to relax.
